If **flare** gives you a different response than expected when using a flag, make sure you're typing `--` (two dashes) and **not** a long dash `—`.

> On **iOS** devices, two dashes can automatically be converted into a long dash, which breaks the command.

### Fix on iOS

To stop your device from auto-replacing dashes:

**Settings** → **General** → **Keyboard** → Turn off **Smart Punctuation**
