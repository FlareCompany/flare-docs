Flare has a built in gacha system linked with the currency. You can collect and sell cards to grow your wealth.
By using flare gacha, you accept our game rules.

# Rules 

- Any type of cheat is strictly forbidden (alting, bypassing limits...) and will result in a blacklist of the gacha.
- Game is game, if you lose anything accidentally you can't get it back (except technical problems)

# Basic guide

Some commands to not miss when you start:

- `search` - Search or look up cards
- `drop` - Drop gacha cards
- `collection` - View card collection
- `cooldown` - Check your drop and claim cooldowns
- `view` - View a card
- `inventory` - View Inventory
- `daily` - Claim your daily reward
- `tag` - Tag a card with an emoji
- `recycle` - Recycle a card for coins
